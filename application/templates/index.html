Our Story

Learn Hack was created by two brothers, Harrison and Charlie Boyns. The young (ish) men in the photo!

Brothers

Learn Hack was created by two brothers, Harrison and Charlie Boyns. The young (ish) men in the photo on the right! Harrison Boyns holds a 1st class degree in Neuroscience and a Masters in Computer Science. In contrast, his brother Charlie studied Law.

Motivation

After finishing University, Harrison went on to work for an International bank, whilst Charlie chose an International consulting firm. During this time, both brother’s recognised the increasing professional demand for coders.

Practice

Fuelled by this knowledge, the pair set about on a mission - a mission to equip the world with the skills of the future!
